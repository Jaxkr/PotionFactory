About Potion Factory

FAQs

  • What is this?

    • It's a game about making potions.

  • How do I play?

    • Press "Turn Crank" to generate energy, then you can go from there.

  • What are golems?

    • A golem is an animated clay construct. In Potion Factory, they are your loyal servants. Your number of golems is the number of actions you can perform simultaneously.

  • What does "Automate" do?

    • The Automate button consumes one golem and automatically performs that action / crafts that recipe once per second.

  • What do the asterisks around the name in the title mean (*Potion Factory*)?

    • This means the game is not currently in focus in your browser, and your resource gathering / crafting MAY be slowed slightly due to browser throttling. I've done my best to avoid this though.

  • Is this open source?

    • Yeah. https://github.com/Jaxkr/PotionFactory
